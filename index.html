About Me

I specialize in quantitative remote sensing and vegetation radiative transfer modeling, focusing on vegetation structural and functional traits. My research merges the latest remote sensing technologies with ecological insights to improve vegetation monitoring and climate science, with a focus on canopy and soil analysis.
