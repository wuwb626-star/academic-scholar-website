About Us

Our group focuses on vegetation remote sensing, exploring how satellite and near-surface observations can reveal the structure, dynamics, and functions of terrestrial ecosystems. We aim to understand vegetation responses to environmental changes, develop advanced retrieval algorithms, and support applications in ecology, agriculture, and resource management.

Research Focus

  • Fine-scale modeling and retrieval of vegetation parameters from UAV and satellite data.
  • Quantifying ecosystem dynamics under global environmental change.
  • Integration of remote sensing, radiative transfer, and deep learning approaches.
  • Applications in natural resource surveys, crop monitoring, and ecological assessment.
