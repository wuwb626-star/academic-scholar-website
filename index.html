About Me

I specialize in quantitative remote sensing and vegetation radiative transfer modeling, focusing on vegetation structural and functional traits. My research combines cutting-edge remote sensing technologies with ecological insights to advance vegetation monitoring and climate science.

Research Interests

  • Retrieval of Vegetation Structure and Functional Traits
