Professional Profile

Peiqi Yang is a recipient of the National High-level Young Talent Program, Jiangsu Distinguished Professor, and Nanjing Normal University Outstanding Young Scholar. His research focuses on quantitative remote sensing, radiative transfer modeling, chlorophyll fluorescence remote sensing, and biogeochemical cycling. He has published over 30 papers in journals including Remote Sensing of Environment and Geoscientific Model Development, established a unified radiative transfer theory across optical, thermal, and fluorescence domains, and developed the SPART soil-vegetation-atmosphere model, mSCOPE, and SCOPE2.0 models. He has also revealed physical relationships between fluorescence and near-infrared reflectance, proposed the R2F relationship theory, and established the FCVI index for plant physiological remote sensing.
