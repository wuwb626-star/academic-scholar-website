Professional Profile

Peiqi Yang is a recipient of the National High-level Young Talent Program, Jiangsu Distinguished Professor, and Nanjing Normal University Outstanding Young Scholar. His research focuses on quantitative remote sensing, radiative transfer modeling, chlorophyll fluorescence remote sensing, and biogeochemical cycling. He has published over 30 papers ...
